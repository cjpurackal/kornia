kornia.sensors.camera
======================

.. currentmodule:: kornia.sensors.camera

:mod:`kornia.sensors.camera` aims to define camera models with flexibility and differentiability in mind.
Camera models available in Kornia are as follows:

#. Pinhole.
#. Brown Conrady.(coming soon)
#. Kannala Brandt K3.(coming soon)
#. Orthographic.(coming soon)
